KYLO GOES UNDERCOVER

Kylo Ren changed out of his intimidating attire swapping his robe for a high visibility jacket and his lightsaber for a wrench as he went undercover on Starkiller Base.

Kylo transforms into Matt, a Radar Technician who chows down with his colleagues and gets his hands dirty fixing some equipment

In this special episode of Undercover Boss find out what people really think of the the First Order and their engimatic commander. Will he be able to blend in or will he stand out like a Wookie on Endor?
