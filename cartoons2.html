KYLO & HOBBES

If you love Star Wars and you love Calvin & Hobbes
(which is everyone right) you are in for a treat.

Super awesome Brian Kesinger a story artist for Marvel
and Disney Animation Studios drew these mashup comics which
takes the characters from a galaxy far away and injects that
Calvin & Hobbes charm.
